Our mission

Say goodbye to exhaustive searches and connect effortlessly with like-minded players. Join a vibrant community where your voice matters—shape the future of tabletop gaming with us!

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do I have to pay for an account?

A: No! Creating an account is completely free. TTT only takes a small cut from those who are hosing paid-for games.

Q: Can I play if I’m under 18?

A: Yes! If you’re under 18 you can join any group that is not marked as mature (18+).

Q: What if I’m being harassed or bullied in my group?

A: Although we hope most players are able to be respectful, in severe cases of harassment or bullying, you can report a specific user to our moderation team. If users are found to be harassing or bullying other players, they will be banned from the site.
